Student Life Development

  • Rachel Elkins

    Campus Involvement: National Panhellenic Council

    Leadership Experience: President Pi Beta Phi,Member of WVU Honors College

    Reelkins1



    • Rachel Elkins is a junior television journalism students with minors in leadership studies and women and gender studies. She is from Lincoln County, W.Va. Rachel is currently the president of the West Virginia Alpha chapter of the Pi Beta Phi sorority. Rachel is a member of the WVU Honors College and is a Neil S. Bucklew Scholar. She also works for WWVU 92, WVU’s student radio station, where she works as a news anchor and a host for the talkshow Feedback. Rachel is running on a platform of Student Life Development. Rachel plans to work closely with Student Life to help refine Greek Life and other student organizations on campus. One of Rachel’s goals includes refining and making the Student Life social policy more accessible to all students and posting it online.
