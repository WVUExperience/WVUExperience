    Noah McDill



    • Noah R. McDill is a dreamer, idealist and a freshman studying political science. Noah is a first generation college student from Hedgesville, W.Va. He is involved on campus in Student Government Association, UNICEF, Oxfam, Chinese Club, the leadWELL mentor program, Honors Hall Council and the Honors College. His favorite moments in WVU have been discovering new limits, whether they are in the Rec Center, the classroom or just simply in everyday life. Noah is also something of a comic book super hero fanatic because he enjoys the idea that one individual can have a positive impact on numerous others. Noah’s platform is Professional Development & Growth. Many people come to college not knowing what they want to do for a career or they have a very vague idea in mind. Noah plans to expose the student body to a number of career possibilities and to bring in guest speakers that will provide details about their jobs along with the choices that brought them to their positions. Along with these events, Noah will also promote the Career Services Center as it is an under-utilized resource capable of readying many students for their futures. His final goal will be to promote research as it is an unknown necessity for many undergraduates that plan to move on to higher education. It is important to understand the vastness of the job market and Noah hopes to help create a path for students to discover and follow their dreams.
