  • Karen Laska

    Campus Involvement: Student Government Association Intern, WVU Food Recovery Network, UNICEF, French Club, and Community Service Club

    Leadership Experience: LEADwell Mentor and Honors Hall Council

    miss_aLASKA_



    • Karen Laska is a freshman international studies major. Karen is from Wheeling, W.Va. Since arriving at WVU, Karen has become involved with the Student Government Association by serving as an intern. She has been able to take an active role in her Residence Hall as a leadWELL mentor and a member of the Honors Hall Council. She is also a member of the WVU Food Recovery Network, the Oxfam, UNICEF, French, and Community Service clubs. Before becoming a Mountaineer, Karen competed several times in the Scripps National Spelling Bee, and her favorite word is triskaidekaphobia. Karen’s platform is Student Safety & Awareness. Karen plans to promote and make University programs such as The Green Dot program more accessible to students. She also plans to work in partnership with Morgantown City Council to install lighting on the Rail Trail.
