Student Information & Communication

  • Matt Rogers

    Campus Involvement: Re-founding of the fraternity Delta Tau Delta, created Paintball Team, Road Chairman and is currently the New Member Educator at Delta Tau Delta

    Leadership Experience: Team Captain and Coach of Paintball Team Club

    Roger_That2012



    • Matt Rogers is a junior geology major from Baltimore, Md. Upon arriving at West Virginia University in the fall of 2012, Matt has been involved with the creation of two different organizations on campus, including the university paintball team and the re-founding of the fraternity Delta Tau Delta. Matt served as a team captain and coach in the founding year of the WVU paintball team. The team participated in its first tournament at Liberty University and was one win shy of moving on to the next round of play. Matt also served as the road chairman and is currently the new member educator at Delta Tau Delta, an organization committed to changing the view of Greek Life on the campus of WVU. Matt’s platform is Student Information & Communication. Matt plans to create a map of all available on-campus parking – noting the restrictions, price and location of each lot. Matt also plans to work in conjunction with various University entities to refine the WVU Alert system. He also plans to promote the city of Morgantown’s alert system to ensure students receive the up-to-date information they need regarding their safety.
