Everyone's College Experience is Different

Your time as a Mountaineer is not limited to what you do in the classroom, on the field or in the residence halls. Your WVU Experience is open to infinite possibilities. The Experience is a team of passionate students ready to challenge the status quo and take Student Government at WVU to the next level. We understand that SGA can no longer limit itself to only part of your college experience. It takes a team of students passionate about your academic progression, your professional connections, your social engagement and your overall well-being to fully encompass the WVU Experience. You’re unique, we're unique and together we create the best WVU Experience.

Why we’re doing it:

As a WVU student, Student Government serves as your voice. When the going gets tough, SGA stands up and goes to bat for students. You can’t truly represent students by focusing on only a few aspects of their college experience. No two students are the same, so why should you be represented as so?

We’re ready to be your voice – in every aspect of your college experience. To do so, it takes a group that is accountable, bold, brave and ready to be the change. Your experience at WVU is what you make of it, take a look around at our site and you’ll see we’re ready to help.
