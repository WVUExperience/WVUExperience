Joseph McGuire

Textbook Affordability & Accessibility

  • Joey McGuire

    Campus Involvement: Active member in the "Pride of West Virginia” Mountaineer Marching Band and Global Medical Brigades

    Leadership Experience: Services on Common Read Committee

    josephmcguirewv



    • Joseph McGuire is freshman biology major. Joseph is from Philippi, W.Va., which lies about an hour south of Morgantown. He has been involved with “The Pride of West Virginia:” The Mountaineer marching band since his enrollment, and is now an active member of both Alpha Lambda Delta and the Global Medical Brigades. Some of Joseph’s most memorable times at WVU so far have been traveling with “The Pride” to Atlanta for the Chick-fil-A Kickoff Classic game and to Memphis, Tennessee for the AutoZone Liberty Bowl Game. In his free time, Joseph likes to relax with his friends and his girlfriend. Upon graduating, Joseph would like to attend medical school. Joseph’s platform is Textbook Affordability. He feels that as college students, it is often hard to afford everything that we need. The current outrageous prices of textbooks don’t help that. By implementing a program where one can get his or her books paid for through work and a program where textbooks can be donated back to the library and used by any student, we can start to address the nation-wide problem of textbook affordability. Together, we can make college more affordable.
