    • Adam Herrick is a senior psychology major. Herrick is originally from Medford, Oregon, and became a resident of Morgantown in 2011. Adam’s favorite past time is rowing, and along with being a member of the West Virginia University Men’s Rowing team, became the President of the Men’s Rowing team. Adam has also prided himself on his mustache, winning West Virginia University’s “Best Mustache” contest in 2013 Adam’s platform is Club Sport Resourcing. Through this, Adam hopes to improve transportation, affordability, and communication to students about Club Sports in general.
