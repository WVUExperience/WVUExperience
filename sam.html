  • Sam Richardson

    WVUSam



    • Sam Richardson is a multidisciplinary studies major with minors in public relations, communication studies, and leadership. Sam is from Rochester, N.Y., and is a proud brother of Sigma Nu Fraternity. He also founded the WVU chapter of the Order of Omega, served as the 2013-14 Student Government Chief of Staff, and currently serves as the Chief of Staff for the Mountaineer Maniacs. Sam recalls the most fun he has ever had at West Virginia University was attending a camping trip with the Outdoor Recreation Center during fall break. Sam’s platform is “Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.” He plans to lead and inspire students at WVU to reduce waste and energy usage. Sam will take initiatives to get students to reduce their total energy consumption and waste by turning their phones on airplane mode, utilizing public transportation, and purchasing electronic textbooks. This will benefit the students economically and serve to preserve our planet. Furthermore, Sam plans to work with the Office of Sustainability to introduce more convenient drop-off locations for recycling clothes, old electronics, batteries, books, and school supplies (such as within Residence Halls). He also strives to create changes to the West Virginia University Student Government Association’s structure that ensures a strong relationship between the student body and the Office of Sustainability.
