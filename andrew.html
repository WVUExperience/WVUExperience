Andrew Sutherland

Community Connections & Tradition

  • Andrew Sutherland

    SGA Experience: Current member of the Athletic Council, former Director of Residential Affairs

    Campus Involvemen: Member Student Advocates for Legislative Advancement, member of the National Society of Collegiate Scholars, former member of the WVU wrestling team.

    TheOneCaptain



    • Andrew Sutherland is a junior political science and communication studies major with a minor in leadership. Andrew is a former WVU wrestler, likes long walks on the beach, food, howling at the moon and a good buddy cop movie. Andrew formerly served as Director of Residential Affairs, and is a current West Virginia University Student Government Association Athletic Councilor. As Athletic Councilor, Andrew worked this year on Rec Center issues and concerns, expansion of the Rec Center, was one of the team captains for 2014 Gold Rush Shirt sales, and helped the Student Government Association and the Maniacs with the search for the development of safer post game traditions. Andrew is also part of the National Society of Collegiate Scholars, which is avid in community service. During his time at West Virginia University, Andrew has made many favorite memories. Andrew’s first favorite memory was his first WVU football game where the team played and beat Marshall. Andrew recalls that as the moment he realized that WVU is bigger than just a school – it is a giant family. Andrew’s platform involves Recreation, Intramurals, Club, and Collegiate sports, or Safety, Community and Tradition. Andrew wants to develop collegiate sports traditions in association with the Mountaineer Maniacs, club sports relations with the community, an intramural charity competition similar to the Bob Huggins’ 3v3 tournament, and continue with the development of the Rec center, especially in the area of safety.
