    RealTalkMorlock



    • Chris Morlock is a current graduate student pursuing a master’s degree in public administration with an emphasis in healthcare administration. Chris is from Bunker Hill, W.Va. Chris is active as a trumpet player in “The Pride of West Virginia:” The Mountaineer marching band, and a brother of Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia Fraternity. Chris is an intern for Congressman David McKinley’s Morgantown office. Chris was one of five finalists for the title of Mr. Mountaineer in 2014. Chris’ platform is Student Safety and Alcoholism Prevention. He wants to ensure safety is a top priority at WVU and hopes to help implement a policy that grants anyone seeking medical attention due to alcohol consumption
