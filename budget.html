The Experience Budget

Our campaign has been funded through donations and personal scholarship money from Stephen and Braxton. We are so fortunate to have friends and family members who believe in our mission and have support us throughout this entire process. Stephen and Braxton elected to use personal scholarship money to help fund the campaign, rather than solicit money from others. Every dollar raised for our campaign comes straight from ourselves and those closest to us. We don’t believe in soliciting for money, simply seeking support from those who have always believed in us.

We have spent, and are continuing to spend a portion of our campaign funds on providing programming during the campaign. Our T-shirts and campaign materials are also items that we purchased with the intent to give back to the students. This means, a portion of our budget is going back to the student body at events we have put on to better serve them. We purchased T-shirts and materials with giving back to students in mind. We will continue to post our campaign expenditures as they are finalized.
